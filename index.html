Wangdu Duntak A Front-End Developer, who loves what he does While Working to Change the Web by Design.

Hi, my name is Wangdu Duntak and I am passionate about creating awesome web applications with an emphasis on thoughtful designs. I am the kind of person who will obsess over the placement of each and every pixel layed out across a page. I am a believer in creating intriguing and enhanced user interactions. With the increase of mobile users, I believe it is important to prototype websites that are responsive and functional across browsers.

I am a recent alumni of Bitmakers Lab, an immersive program in web development focusing on building applications using Ruby on Rails. The program taught me to emphasize best practices of writing code such as understanding architectural patterns like MVC, CRUD and RESTful APIs.

I am always exploring new frameworks and packages that will help me setup the best stacks for my projects. Recently, I have been adventuring in the world of MeteorJS and concurrently MongoDB to launch reactive apps.

Currently, I reside in Toronto, Canada. I am looking for opportunities for full-time employment but at the same time, I am open to accepting contract work.
